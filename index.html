What is this?

In The Black Swan, Nassim Taleb describes two societies, mediocristan and extremistan.

In Mediocristan, wealth is distributed normally, with extremes being highly unlikely.
In Extremistan, wealth is distributed more like a power-law, with the top 1% holding the vast majority of wealth.
This page attempts to visualize those two societies! The key is that wealth is represented as weight. Each circle represents a person, with the number being their weight (aka 'wealth'). Toggle between the two and see the difference!

Note: The data is being conjured arbitrarily. My next goal is to pull data from a real-world source (census data).

More details: Toggling Extremistan creates the '1%' and '99%' - so that 1% of your population size will become super wealthy, and the other 99% remain 'average'.
